Empowering Users to Build a Safer Online Space

Red Flag Social is a platform dedicated to promoting online safety and user trust. We empower users to report and identify instances of scams or abuse linked to social media handles.

How it Works:

  • Simply enter a social media username in the search bar on our homepage.
  • Our system will search for any reports filed against that handle.
  • If reports are found, you'll see a summary of the reported issues.
  • (Optional) You can also choose to create an account to submit your own reports and contribute to a safer online community.

Benefits:

  • Protect Yourself: Make informed decisions before interacting with potentially risky social media accounts.
  • Help Others: Report suspicious activity and warn others about potential scams or abuse.
  • Build Trust: Contribute to a more accountable and trustworthy online environment.
